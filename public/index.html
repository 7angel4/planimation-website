Planiwiki is an extension to the Planimation webpage hosted on GitHub. It features both documentations on important functions and properties, as well as a gallery containing various sample animations. It aims to allow visitors to truly appreciate the magnificence of Planimation, as well as to promote Planimation among students and researchers in the field of artificial intelligence.
