General structure of an Animation Profile¶

:effect Functions¶

Distribution of objects¶

Distribution functions 'distribute' objects in a certain manner. They are used to lay objects out on the screen. All objects for which the same parameters of 'distribute' are called will be included in the distribution. Distribution works such that objects will be placed in a certain area without overlapping. Between animation frames, objects will not move (unless their positions are reset).

distributex

Distributes objects along a horizontal plane.

distributey

Distributes objects along a vertical plane.

distribute_grid_around_point

Distributes objects within a grid-like structure.

distribute_within_objects_vertical

Distributes objects vertically within the bounds of another object.

distribute_within_objects_horizontal

Distributes inner objects horizontally within the bounds of another outer object.
Objects will shift to the left when an empty space is made available.

distribute_within_objects_horizontal_v2

Distributes inner objects horizontally within the bounds of another outer object.
When an inner object leaves, the space is reserved and will be allocated to the same object upon return.

Other functions¶

calculate_label

Counts the number of objects being 'labelled' by this function for another object.

align_middle

Aligns an object to the middle of another.

apply_smaller

Sets y to be displayed smaller than x.

draw_line

Draws a line between two objects.
